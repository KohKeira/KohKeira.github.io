Experiences

My journey starts here...

Being part of the EXCO

As a member of the Executive Committee, I was involved in many projects. For example, my team had to plan for major events like Teachers' Day Celebration. I learnt the importance of teamwork through the many collaborations and I am inspired to serve the community.

Being a volunteer

I enjoy spending time with the elderly at the old folks home as they remind me of my grandparents. To me, it is my pleasure to be able to give back to society, no matter how limited my ability is. It is rewarding to see the smiles on their faces, knowing that we have given them happiness.

Being part of the International Chess Club

In my first year, I had the chance to be a representative of my CCA. It was a memorable experience as it was the only Open House I participated offline. I have honed my skills to be able to interact with parents and my future schoolmates. Some of them even joined Ngee Ann because of Chess Club!

Being part of the International Chess Club

Every year, I have the opportunity to represent my school in many chess events. I like to challenge myself and embrace them with an open mind. Chess requires strong mental resilience, the ability to remain composed even if I am losing. I have dealt with stress countless times to emerge victorious.

Being exposed to technology

From young, I have always had an interest in technology. I learnt how to do Python programming after taking up Computing in Secondary 3. Apart from that, I also participated in the AI internship by Temasek Polytechnic. Technology is very useful in our everyday lives, and this has motivated me to work hard and join this industry in the future.
